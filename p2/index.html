About Me

Hello everyone! I'm Wen-Chung Cheng, but you can call me Andy. I graduated from FAU in Summer 2017 with a dual BS degree in Physics and Mathematics. Then I took several years off pondering upon my future and did some mission trips to Haiti and Colombia. Now I'm a Computer Science Major starting this Summer. I'm taking this class to complete the core requirement for the Computer Science degree. I am currently working as a research assistant in the CS department at FAU where Dr. Zhen Ni supervises my research on automatic parking/charging using machine learning in robotics. I am taking this class because I might design a web tool to make checking the status of parking spots more convenient and easier. When I'm not focusing on school and computers, I enjoy doing activities involving music such as playing in the worship team at Spanish River Church, and playing piano. I used to design my dad’s company website for fun so I am very excited to have a class where I can advance my knowledge! I look forward to work with you guys as a group in this online class!
